Our Main Objective

service

Camp educators, program faculty and local artists lead classes and workshops. The focus is on experiential and hands-on learning. Service projects are organized in collaboration with the camps stellar educators and local communities.

leadership development

CORE classes are centered on leadership and ethics. Participants have an opportunity to explore local culture through workshops, including chess, dance, theatre and visual arts.

cultural exchange

Participants will experience local life and culture through delicious cuisine, know about our historical places.
